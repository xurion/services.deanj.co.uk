About

Dean James Services offer a multitude of web solutions.

We register all domain types, from .com and .co.uk, to .io, .london and .lol. As standard, our web hosting package is unlimited, so you don't need to worry about running of out space if your website grows in size.

We also offer great email solutions, whether you need dedicated email addresses or simple mail forwarders that send on your email to your own Gmail or any other existing email address.
